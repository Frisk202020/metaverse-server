Who are we ?

I'm Clover SPYRROW, chief of an investigation team of the Government that aims to destroy the UNDERDOGS from the inside.

In order to infiltrate, we need to gather all the information we can on their organization, and we're collecting every illegal IP in order to map the metaverse. That's when you can help us. If you have any information that could be useful for us, please head to the report center section !


The spying department

The department is divided in three subsections that are each specialized on a peculiar task

  • Infiltration section : people specialized in undercover missions
  • Data collection section : people specialized at retrieving confidential information from UNDERDOGS
  • Engineering section : people that develop minimalist tools utilized by other sections. For difficult tasks, we may ask to the coding department

My team

My team belongs to the data collection section, we collect all the data we find about UNDERDOGS, that we retrieve using different strategies :

  • Interception : we can retrieve messages sent between UNDERDOGS that are poorly secured
  • Searching : we look at every terminal found by us or the surveillance department, and we exploit all the data and terminal links we find to expand our map of UNDERDOGS
  • Infiltration assistance : we help undercover agents by providing useful information about the situation they're put into
